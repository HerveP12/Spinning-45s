Spinning 45s Rules

How it works: Place a bet on the "Spinning 45s" bet spot. A counter starts at 0 and increases with the numbers spun after each round.

Payouts:

  • Spin "0" or "00": Receive 2x your bet, and the counter remains active.
  • Counter reaches exactly 45: Bet pays out at 10-1.
  • Counter exceeds 45: The bet is lost, and the counter resets.

Key Details: The bet remains active until the counter either reaches or exceeds 45.

Examples:

  1. Example 1:
    • Bet: $5
    • Spin: 17 (Counter = 17). Bet still active.
    • Spin: "00" (Counter = 17). Player receives $10. Bet still active.
    • Spin: 21 (Counter = 38). Bet still active.
    • Spin: 10 (Counter = 48). Bet lost.
  2. Example 2:
    • Bet: $5
    • Spin: 5 (Counter = 5). Bet still active.
    • Spin: 30 (Counter = 35). Bet still active.
    • Spin: 10 (Counter = 45). Player wins $50.
